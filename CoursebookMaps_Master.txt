================================================================
DIFFERENTIATION ASSISTANT — COURSEBOOK KNOWLEDGE BASE
================================================================
This document contains structured content maps for all coursebooks
used in Cyprus secondary schools. When a teacher selects a coursebook
and unit, search this document for the relevant entry and use the
data to generate aligned, differentiated activities.

To locate a unit: search for the COURSEBOOK name + UNIT number.
Example: "OWN IT 1 | UNIT 3" or "ODF LEVEL 3 | UNIT 7"
